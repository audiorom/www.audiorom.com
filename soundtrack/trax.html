Sound Track

Sound Track is a multi user networked installation which enables an audience to construct orchestral arrangements from iconographic images. Using the train line between 2 city's as its source, Sound Track looks at industrial and electronic systems such as trains and computers to explore the notion of physical and digital space. Placing the emphasis firmly on the sonic in the case of Sound Track, visuals will be drawn from railway archives and time tabling information in order to create a new form of musical notation.

This is an extension of AudioRom's characteristic fusion of sound and visuals as the artists continue to place themselves as one of the most innovative collectives working on this type of synergy.
