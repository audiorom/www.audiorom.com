AudioRom and Simon Schofield were invited to hold a workshop and seminar on interactive music as part of the Digital Dreams Conference.



The workshop was made up of 10 delegates who contributed, recorded images and sounds from around Newcastle using a video camera .The rushes were then digitalised into still images and sounds for importing into a director interface.

The interface represented a cityscape that could import the images as city icons which would play the digitalised sounds as you navigated around the city interface.

Unfortunately at the seminar we were unable to demonstrate the work collated from the workshop as our Mac refused to load the city interface. So we took the opportunity to show AudioRom.
