Trigger Happy

Trigger Happy has toured round the globe since its creation, making it one of AudioRoms most successful installations. This installation allows up to 11 participants to mix music together by interacting with light sensors. The viewers can drop in various musical passages and samples at their own chosen points in the track which animates the visual content within the work. Viewers are essentially choosing their own tracks and sequencing them live into the system using a similar approach to a DJ / VJ.

Trigger Happy has last been shown at Kunst+Technik in Berlin in September.
