Shift Control

Released in March 98, This Enhanced CDROM profiles 40 minutes of AudioRom's Post Drum and Bass along with 2 guest tracks from one of UK's best underground dance acts T Power.
The enhanced cd is both MAC and WINDOWS compatible and contains 16 impressive musical interfaces each providing a playful and compelling way of interacting with the music.
Using forms such as typing or game play, AudioRom establish the relationship to musical arrangement equipping the user with hundreds of possible re-combinations while maintaining harmony and rhythm.
