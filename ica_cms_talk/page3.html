Enter the web CPS toolkit

What does a CPS toolkit contain ?

  • A datastore for content/structure (image, articles, content of navigation bars)
  • or an interface to an established datastore (ie. a RDMS)
  • A template language to pull content from the datastore
  • Workflow - legal, sub-edit
  • Staging - Live/Production environments
  • Locking - collaborative working
  • Caching scheme
  • Editors tools - article editor, image management tools, page construction tools, construction of navbars
