Multimedia: Virtual Philosophers

Article from: Creative Review

November 1996 Volume 16 No.11 P35

by Liz Faber

"Sunbather is currently distributing a CD-Plus which it produced for a techno outfit called AudioRom. The disc contains three music tracks and around a dozen sound toys based on their music. A full version of AudioRom will be released next year with a link to the web that allows the user to up-load sounds and jam with people online."
