On each day of the event a different, market leading, provider of development tools, will host a user forum aimed at current and future users. This will consolidate the profile of the show, adding to the quantity and quality of the Multimedia '96 audience.

Exhibitors will benefit through the increased traffic from high profile, high spending cross media specialists visiting these sessions.

The user forum and the seminar programme will make Multimedia '97 a multi-faceted, educational, informative and inspiring event combining a tools-led exhibition with a strong educational experience.

All features subject to change at the discretion of the organisers.
