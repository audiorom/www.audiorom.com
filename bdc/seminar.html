Written by Sue Weekes, editor of Creative Technology, the seminar programme investigates the real issues surrounding the production, development and publishing of multimedia and the different skills required for cross media implementation.
The seminar programme will focus on producing better quality, more accessible multimedia content. Increasingly, creative experts in linear fields are becoming involved in the production of new media.

Three sessions a day throughout the show will target producers and developers of multimedia products in addition to producers of content components.

All features subject to change at the discretion of the organisers.
