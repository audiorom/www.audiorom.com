August 96 was the media schmooze for AudioRom Beta 1 held in Soho, London. We ran a three data projected presentation of AudioRom, where "party goers" after filling up on the free booze were able to mix between machines. The evening was a success, thanks to all that came to see and hear the work! Watch this space for news on the launch for AudioRom 1 the commercial release.
