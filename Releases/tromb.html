This area is for downloading some of the individual toys from AudioRom Beta1 to your desktop.

They have been made as dcr files to save download time so they can only be viewed in a browser that has a shockwave plugin. Each toy has help text built into the interface.

To navigate around this area rollover the strings to play the toy, hold the mouse down and drag over a string to trigger the page.
