Sound Track

Sound Track was originally a multi user networked installation which enabled an audience to construct orchestral arrangements from iconographic images. Using the train line between 2 city's as its source, Sound Track looks at industrial and electronic systems such as trains and computers to explore the notion of physical and digital space. Placing the emphasis firmly on the sonic, visuals are drawn from railway archives and time tabling information in order to create a new form of musical notation.

A CD-Rom will be released in spring 1999.
