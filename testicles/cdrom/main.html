CD-Roms
As the name suggests AudioRom makes interactive music cd-roms. To date there are three releases.
The very first one was an ambitious college project by Andre Ktori. A 1000 copy strong edition of 'beta1' was given away for free.
In March 1998 'Interactive Music Shift Control' was released and is now globally distributed.
AudioRom 0 is about to be pressed and will be given away for free, thanks to the Arts Council of England.
