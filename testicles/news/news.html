ISEA'98

Sound Track
AudioRom will be Manchester and Liverpool from the 3rd to 6th of September to present Sound Track to the people. Networked computers placed in boxes on the stations in Manchester and Liverpool let you arrange and mix an orchestral/industrial one hour long piece of music. You are invited to come along at any time from 10.30am to 6pm.

Audioroom
An Installation which shows extracts from the AudioRom ShiftControl in the context of home entertainment and living in the future. This exhibition has been running since 23rd May and finishes on 13th September. It includes seat designs from Marc Newson and Euro Lounge.
Co-commissioned by FACT and The Museum of Science and Industry.
