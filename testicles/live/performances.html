Performances

Interactive Performance DEAF (Dutch Electronic Arts Festival) '96 in 'Night Town' Rotterdam

Using a number of sensory devices such as video tracking, laser sensors and audio responsive systems, the dancing audience was able to trigger off pre-authored images and sounds. Using five data projectors, the content was placed onto screens constructed in the form of a dome. The dome was suspended over the dance floor, creating an imersive environment.

Performance at the Festival Nouveaux de Cinema et Nouveaux Medias '97 (FCMM) in Montreal

This event linked venues in Barcelona, London, Montreal and users at home. The performance used video conferencing, live internet relay chat and live FTP technology to allow a remote audience to upload images, sounds and text. Accompanied by preauthored images, digital video and live audio they were all composed together on one large projected screen to create a live telematic jam/performance broadcasted on the internet and at the venues. AudioRom took an editing role by censoring and mixing the content into a coherent form.

Performance at Fashionshow in Kolding/Copenhagen '98

Two projection screens on either side of the catwalk were filled with alternating, interactive musical interfaces. The music from the different sound toys was mixed and anranged live to provide an audio-visual multimedia performance that enriched and complimented the overall experience of the fashioshow.
