HARP&PIANO
SONGS
is a CD and an instrumental concert for harp and piano


Harp & Piano, reached the second album production with BMG, has an high quality project, different from usual proposal: musical background of the artist, who made classic studies and their virtuosisms are shaping in melodic and pop songs, balacing and floating in this land of and millenium.
A cross-over coming from deep desire to express instrumental music merged with pop-echoes, with riythms and world sounds and also mixed to a musical languages having classic and mimimal roots to create and elegant sound impact.

Andrea Griminelli, as special guest played in cd SONGS a piece written for this occasion by Marco Betta, called Andria.

H&P has been invited to play in the soundtrack of the latest movie produced by Giuseppe Tornatore, that will be distribuited in year 2000.
Cd SONGS is the the anticipation of incoming CD related to to the soundtrack.

H&P made exibitions at Lingotto di Torino, Teatro Massimo in Palermo, Nuovo Piccolo Teatro e Teatro di Porta Romana in Milano, Cantieri Culturali alla Zisa in Palermo where they palyed along with Meira Asher, Tanita Tikaram and Paco De Lucia and besides these they played in other prestigious theatres.
