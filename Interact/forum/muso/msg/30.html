going back to this thing about "why can't we import our own sounds" for a moment;

as an ordinary joe going about his own business with scant regard for cutting-edge music authoring methodologies, I quite like the way the CD doesn't ask for or need any of my own 'sounds' as I ain't got none.
I don't want to buy my little piece of entertainment (which is what this all is -- let's not forget that) and then be asked to do some work in making it myself. Sure, there's a time and a place for that sort of thing (behind closed doors, consenting adults etc etc), but in terms of market share - nada.
I want to be told stories, to be surprised, to be able to play with stories, biuld a little wall of building-blocks and knock it down again. I certainly don't want to make the building blocks themselves.
'S like 'interactive fiction'. Might as well sell me a completely blank exercise book and a pen for £16.99 and tell me to get on with it.

whatever.
