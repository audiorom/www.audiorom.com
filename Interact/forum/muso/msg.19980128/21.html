Posted by mMatt on June 14, 1997 at 4:27:26:

It's all very well
being able to create,
but what do you do
when you want to relate ?

Can these tools really aid us
to build on our dreams.
Or is the illusion,
much further on than it seems.

only those who search,
will find anything,
the rest is left
for dis-covering.
In prose there is comment,
not there to be dormant,

I wake up,
and realise,
that I see
through 'our eyes...............................mM.............
