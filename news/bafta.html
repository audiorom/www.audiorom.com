ShiftControl wins Milia d'Or

February 8th. AudioRom picks up the first award of the evening at the Milia 99 Awards in Cannes.
 ShiftControl was nominated in the Leisure and Entertainment Category along side titles such as Sony PlayStation's Busta Groove out of applications from 57 different countries. Check out the Milia site for more details on the event.

Bafta Awards

Thursday, 29th October:

"Shift Control" gets a BAFTA !

AudioRom performed their last live event for '98 at the Interactive BAFTA Awards Ceremony at the Inter-Continental Hotel.

The ceremony opened with a blast of sounds and images in a five minute long interactive audio-visual performance, with a line-up of four drummers and a new console commissioned by BAFTA and ICL. AudioRom triggered images and video jointly created by the Butler Brothers and Hextatic.
